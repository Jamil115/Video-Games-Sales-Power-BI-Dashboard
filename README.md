# Video Game Sales Dashboard  

This project is an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that provides insights into the global video game sales dataset. The dashboard highlights sales trends, most popular genres, top publishers and developers, and regional market performance.  

---

## Key Features
- Total Games and Genres – Overview of 1,210 games across 18 different genres  
- Highest-Selling Platforms – Analysis of top-selling games and their critic scores  
- Favourite Genres – Distribution of most preferred video game genres  
- Published Games by Publisher – Identifies leading publishers such as Electronic Arts, Nintendo, and Ubisoft  
- Published Games by Developer – Highlights top developers like Capcom, EA Canada, and Konami  
- Regional Sales Breakdown – Compares Japanese, North American, European & African, and other regional sales  
- Games Released vs. Sales by Year – Tracks how sales and game releases evolved over time  

---

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation – Preparing dataset for analysis  
- Business Intelligence – Extracting and presenting industry insights  

---

## Insights
- **Electronic Arts** leads in publishing with 115 released games  
- **Capcom** is the top developer with 40 published games  
- Action and Shooter are among the most popular video game genres  
- Sales peaked in the mid-2000s, followed by a decline in later years  
- Regional sales patterns vary, with North America and Europe dominating the market
